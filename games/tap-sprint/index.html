    Test Your Reaction Time with Tap Sprint

    Looking for a challenging reaction time browser game? Tap Sprint is designed to push your reflexes to the limit. The rules are simple: hit the target tap count before the timer burns out. It is an intense click speed test game that requires no downloads and plays instantly directly in your browser.

    How to improve your tapping speed?

    To master this fast tap game online, keep your mouse hand relaxed and stay focused on the center of the screen. Consistent practice on Tap Sprint serves as an excellent daily brain warm-up to train your raw reaction speed.
