How to Play

  1. 1 Read the grid quickly and look for the only tile with a slightly different tone.
  2. 2 Tap the odd tile before the timer expires or the run ends immediately.
  3. 3 Advance through all nine levels as the grid grows and the visual delta tightens.

About this Game

Find the One rewards focus more than speed at first, then gradually forces both. It is BeatTo9's observation challenge for players who enjoy calm pressure and visual scanning.

    Tip 1 Scan wide
